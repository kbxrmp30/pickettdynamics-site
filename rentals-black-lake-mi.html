About This Property

Overview

Our Black Lake rental is a lakefront cottage in Onaway, Michigan, with direct access to beautiful Black Lake. The cottage sleeps up to 5 guests with 2 bedrooms, 3 beds, and 1 bathroom.

It is a quiet Northern Michigan getaway for fishing, swimming, boating, kayaking, hiking, snowmobiling, and relaxing by the water. Step out to the dock, launch a kayak, or simply watch the lake from the screened sunroom.

Insider Picks

From the Locals

Things worth knowing from people who've spent real time up here.

💧
Ocqueoc Falls
Michigan's largest waterfall — and almost no one outside Northern Michigan knows it exists. Easy 10-minute walk from the parking area. Not crowded. Absolutely worth the drive.
Local Pick
🎣
Black Lake Walleye
Black Lake has a well-earned reputation for walleye. Early morning and late evening are the sweet spots. Pick up a Michigan DNR fishing license before you go — required on the water.
Fishing Tip
🍽️
Cheboygan Waterfront
Worth a stop for lunch or dinner. The waterfront area along the Cheboygan River is a solid afternoon — the town has a relaxed Northern Michigan feel without the tourist crowds.
Local Pick
🍬
Mackinac Island Fudge
When you make the trip to the island, fudge is mandatory. Most shops do samples — try a few before you commit. The island is car-free, so plan to walk or rent a bike.

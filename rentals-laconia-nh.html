About This Property

Overview

Our Laconia rental is an all-season Lakes Region getaway near Lake Winnipesaukee. The home sleeps up to 8 guests with 3 bedrooms, 6 beds, and 3 bathrooms, making it a strong fit for families, couples, or groups looking for lake access, nearby attractions, and a comfortable home base.

Guests have access to a private community beach, and the house is less than 1 mile from Weirs Beach. Whether you're visiting in summer for the lake and boardwalk, fall for the foliage, or winter for Gunstock, this is a year-round home base for the Lakes Region.

Insider Picks

From the Locals

Things worth knowing from people who've spent real time in the Lakes Region.

🍦
Liliuokalani's Ice Cream
A local favorite around the Lakes Region. The kind of place regulars don't advertise. Worth stopping in after a day at the beach.
Local Pick
🎮
Funspot
The world's largest arcade is right here in Weirs Beach — not an exaggeration. Free to walk in, pay per game. A Lakes Region institution since 1952.
Local Pick
⛵
M/S Mount Washington
Day cruises and dinner sailings on Lake Winnipesaukee. The full lake tour runs about 3 hours — a great way to see the whole lake from the water.
Local Pick
🏍️
Laconia Motorcycle Week — Late June
The oldest motorcycle rally in the US. If you're here in late June, Weirs Beach and the Lakes Region turns into something you have to see. Plan for traffic on 3 and 11.

The Process

How a project usually goes

Most projects start with a short conversation. You don't need to have all the details worked out — just a general idea of what you're after is enough to get started.

Once we've talked through the scope, we'll send a quote and timeline. From there it's a straightforward build with occasional check-ins if decisions come up along the way.

  1. 1
    You reach out Email or form — share your idea, size, timeline, and any reference photos you have.
  2. 2
    We talk through the details We'll ask a few follow-up questions and discuss materials, finish, and design direction.
  3. 3
    You receive a quote We'll send a quote with the price and estimated build time for the specific project.
  4. 4
    We build it Once approved, we get to work. You'll hear from us when it's ready for pickup or shipping.

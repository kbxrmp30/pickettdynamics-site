About Pickett Dynamics

Building something worth doing in Acton, MA

Pickett Dynamics is the operating umbrella for a growing set of community ventures founded and run by Matthew Pickett — all rooted in the Acton, MA area and built around the idea that good businesses make their community better.

Each venture starts small, stays local, and grows from genuine interest rather than trend-chasing.

📍
Based in Acton, Massachusetts All ventures are local-first, serving the Acton and surrounding MetroWest community.
🤝
Community-driven Free or accessible programs, honest pricing, and no hidden strings.

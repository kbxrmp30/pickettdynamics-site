The Process

How Custom Projects Work

1

Share Your Idea

Tell us what you want made — size, style, wording, theme, material preference, and where it will be used.

2

Design Review

We'll review the idea, discuss materials and design direction, and confirm whether the project is a good fit.

3

Build & Finish

Once approved, the piece is cut, carved, sanded, finished, and prepared for pickup or delivery.

4

Pickup or Delivery

We'll coordinate pickup, local delivery, or shipping depending on the project size and location.

Get Started

Have an idea for a custom piece?

Send a message with your concept, approximate size, preferred material, deadline, and any reference photos. Most projects start with a rough idea — you don't need to have everything figured out.

Before You Reach Out

Helpful Details to Send

You don't need to have everything figured out before reaching out — a rough idea and a few of these details is enough to get started. The more you can share, the faster we can put together an accurate quote.

  • Approximate dimensions (length, width, height or thickness)
  • Indoor or outdoor placement
  • Preferred wood tone — dark, medium, or light
  • Style preference: modern, rustic, farmhouse, industrial, lake house, etc.
  • Any reference photos or inspiration images
  • Text, logos, or personalization for CNC-carved pieces
  • Desired timeline
  • Budget range, if known
  • Delivery preference — local pickup or shipping

Not sure about any of these? That's fine. The goal is to get the conversation started so we can ask the right questions and help narrow down the design and materials together.

You can email us directly, use the form below, or attach a photo if you have one. A quick photo of the room or space where the piece will go is especially helpful for furniture and shelving projects.

Not sure if your project is a good fit? Send a note anyway. The worst we can say is we're not the right shop for it — but we'll always try to help point you in the right direction.

What We Work With

Materials & Finish Options

Most projects use one or more of these materials. Availability and pricing vary — we'll discuss the best fit for your project when you reach out.

🪵
Walnut
Rich, dark grain. A premium hardwood for furniture, signs, and statement pieces.
🌾
White Oak
Warm, neutral tone with prominent ray-fleck patterning. Very durable.
🍁
Maple
Light, tight grain. Great for painted pieces, CNC work, and cutting boards.
🎨
Painted accents
Milk paint, chalk paint, or chalk finish for signs, frames, and decorative pieces.
💧
Epoxy fill
River-style or accent fills in any color. Adds drama to live-edge and slab pieces.
✨
Clear coat
Satin, semi-gloss, or matte polyurethane. Durable, easy-care finish for daily use pieces.
🌧️
Outdoor-rated finish
Spar urethane or exterior-grade oil for signs, patio pieces, and outdoor furniture.
🔩
Metal legs & hardware
Black powder-coat, raw steel, or brass-finish options for an industrial or modern look.

Good to Know

Important Notes

Pricing is quoted individually. Every project varies significantly by material, size, complexity, and finish. There's no standard price list — every quote is based on the specific project.
Logo and licensed artwork: We can engrave custom text, original artwork, and personal designs. We cannot reproduce trademarked logos, licensed characters, or copyrighted artwork without written permission from the rights holder — regardless of how the request is framed.
Natural wood characteristics — grain variation, knots, checks, color variation, and wood movement — are expected parts of working with real hardwood. These are not defects; they are what make each piece unique.
We'll respond within 2–3 business days to discuss your project, ask follow-up questions, and provide a rough estimate. Complex or large projects may take a bit longer to quote accurately.

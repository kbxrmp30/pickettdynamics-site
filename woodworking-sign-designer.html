Prompt Guide

What to include in your prompt

The more specific your prompt, the more accurate the AI result — and the more clearly we can understand what you want. Include as many of these details as you can:

✏️  Text / wording on the sign
🪵  Wood type (walnut, oak, pine, maple)
🎨  Finish (dark stain, natural, painted fill)
📐  Approximate size (e.g. 24" wide)
🔲  Shape (rectangle, arch-top, circle, irregular)
🖋️  Font style (rustic, script, block, serif)
🌲  Border or design elements (pine trees, waves, wildlife)
📍  Where it will hang (home entry, cabin, garage, outdoor)
Pro tip: End every prompt with "photorealistic product photo, clean white studio background, soft lighting" — it tells the AI to render the sign like a real finished piece sitting on a shelf, which gives you a much better preview of what the actual sign will look like.

About Custom Tables

Overview

A custom coffee table is more than a place to set a drink — it anchors the room. We build custom coffee tables around the size, style, material, and finish that fit your space.

Projects can range from clean modern hardwood tables to rustic slab-style pieces, epoxy-accented tables, or specialty designs. Because each table is custom, pricing is quoted individually based on wood species, size, finish, and design complexity.

The process starts with your room and your vision. Share a few photos and a rough idea of what you're looking for, and we'll help figure out the rest.

Styles

Table Styles

🪵

Modern Walnut Coffee Tables

Clean lines, rich walnut grain, and a smooth protective finish. Works well with modern, mid-century, and Scandinavian interiors.

🌿

Rustic Slab Coffee Tables

Natural-edge slab-style tables with character — knots, checks, and organic shapes. Great for farmhouse, cabin, or lake house settings.

💧

Epoxy River Coffee Tables

Dramatic epoxy channel or inlay — custom color, gloss, and translucency. A bold centerpiece for the right room.

🏠

White Oak or Maple Coffee Tables

Lighter hardwood options for brighter spaces or rooms that call for a warmer, less-dark aesthetic than walnut.

⚙️

Industrial Metal-Leg Tables

Wood top with black steel, welded, or hairpin-style legs. Works well in industrial, modern, and mixed-material interiors.

✏️

Custom Shape or Specialty Tables

Round, hexagonal, live-edge, or non-standard shapes. Subject to design review and material availability.

Before You Reach Out

Good Details to Send

You don't need to have everything figured out — a rough idea and a few of these details is enough to get started.

  • Room dimensions
  • Approximate table size
  • Photos of the room
  • Preferred wood tone (dark, medium, light)
  • Style preference: modern, rustic, farmhouse, industrial, lake house, etc.
  • Epoxy or no epoxy
  • Leg style: wood, black metal, brass, hairpin, X-frame, etc.
  • Desired timeline
  • Budget range, if known
  • Any reference photos or inspiration images

Good to Know

Important Notes

Because custom coffee tables vary significantly by size, material, finish, and design complexity, pricing is quoted individually. There is no standard list price — every quote is based on the specific project.
Large pieces may require local pickup or local delivery. Shipping is available for some projects depending on size and packaging requirements.
Wood movement, grain variation, knots, checks, and color variation are natural characteristics of real hardwood. These are not defects — they are part of what makes each piece unique.

Get Started

Interested in a custom coffee table?

Send the approximate dimensions, a few inspiration photos, and a picture of the room where it will go. We can help narrow down the wood, finish, shape, and design direction.
